BIO

Tasnim Ullah is a creative from Bangladesh — a designer, video creator, and digital innovator who lives at the intersection of art and technology.

What started as a curiosity about visual storytelling quickly evolved into a full-fledged craft. From DaVinci Resolve color grading sessions that stretch into the night, to building AI-integrated pipelines that push the boundaries of what's possible — Tasnim is always in pursuit of the extraordinary.

Driven by a deep belief that creativity is not a talent but a discipline, every project is approached with the philosophy of the void: strip away the noise, find the signal, and let the work speak with unforgettable clarity.
